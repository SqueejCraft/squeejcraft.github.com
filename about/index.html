About SqueejCraft

Welcome to SqueejCraft! We are a Java & Bedrock Minecraft server dedicated to keeping the vanilla lifestyle you know and love!

Our Mission

We're a single server network with a strong focus on server quality and performance without having to strip the game of its vanilla mechanics. SqueejCraft has perfected the art of running a server optimally using some of the best software & hardware available, so you can truly build anything that your heart desires without the fear of lag prevention measures ruining your projects.

Our Team

We have the privilege of maintaining a dedicated staff team that has been here for a long period of time. You'll be safe to know that our staff grew along with the server and have the best interests of the players at heart.

Join Us

Join our community on Discord and visit all our socials to stay up to date on all the recent events, improvements, builds, and community projects so you can have the scoop on what's going on.

Our History

SqueejCraft was founded with the goal of creating a unique Minecraft experience. Over the years, we have grown and evolved, always striving to provide the best possible server for our community. Founded by our owner, vnvxvnv, we originally ran our own hardware and managed everything ourselves. This advantage of 24/7 access to our servers meant we could deliver the advantage of having multiple servers, worlds, unlimited borders and allow for high end server configuration that other hosts don't often support. Now, we host with a Non-Profit company, lowering costs of our monthly payments and ensuring you still have the same enjoyment we originally delivered all while continuously updating our server.

Future Plans

We are constantly working on new features and improvements to make SqueejCraft even better. Stay tuned for exciting updates and announcements! While we stick to the vanilla way of life, you'll notice weekly updates, community projects, and community feedback aimed at getting YOUR view on what you'd like to expect from us.
