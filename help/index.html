Command Description Available to
/spawn Teleports you to the server spawn after 3 seconds. Usable once every 20 seconds Member
/sethome Sets your home location, return to it with the /home command Member
/tpa Allows you to teleport to another player Member
/s Shows the Quick Access Menu Member
/help View general information about our server Member
/trade Gives useful commands on how you can trade remotely with other players Member
/shophelp Displays all commands you can use with our shop plugin Member
/shortcuts View most of our commands and their designated shortcuts for easy access Member
/team Displays information on your team and how to set one up Member
//report Reports what's currently happening in chat to Staff Member

Need assistance?

We are always available on our Discord server if you need assistance. If we are in-game, shoot us a message! Or if anything happens while we're gone in Minecraft, please use //report or let us know on Discord.

Want to become staff?

While we appreciate the need to be Staff, trusted members of our community are in great hands with current Staff Members. We welcome help, input, and criticism, and we are always looking on ways to improve the user experience.

Want to donate?

We're glad you want to donate! Our donation page is located on the top of the webpage. Donations are not required and are non-refundable. In the chance you chargeback, proper consequences will be brought upon your player.
